Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a complaint?
Go to the dashboard and click on "File a Complaint". Fill in the required details and submit.
How can I track my complaint?
Navigate to "My Complaints" and click on any complaint to view its timeline and status.
What does "In Progress" mean?
It means your complaint is currently being worked on by the administration.
Can I edit my complaint after submission?
No, once submitted, complaints cannot be edited to maintain authenticity.
What if my complaint is rejected?
You may submit a new complaint with proper details or contact support for clarification.

Important Guidelines

  • Provide accurate and complete details.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate complaints.
